Tier Testing Process

To begin your tier assessment, join our official Discord server and navigate to the #request-test channel.

A staff member will guide you through the testing process, which typically consists of multiple matches against established tier holders to accurately gauge your skill level.

The testing process is designed to be fair and comprehensive, evaluating not just your mechanical skills but also your game sense, adaptability, and consistency under pressure.

Testing Kit Information

Download the official testing kit to practice the exact scenarios used during tier assessments.

The kit includes custom maps, specific item loadouts, and practice arenas designed to simulate real testing conditions.
